Frequently
asked questions

01

What makes Stinger balls different?

Stinger balls are selected for consistent grip, clean release, and dependable bounce so players can trust the feel across repeated reps.

02

Do you support team and club orders?

Yes. Stinger Lacrosse is built for teams, clubs, camps, clinics, and coaches who need reliable ball supply in practical pack sizes.

03

Are the balls good for training?

Absolutely. They are made for wall ball, shooting reps, passing drills, ground balls, warmups, and daily team practices.

04

Can I request bulk pricing?

Yes. Use the contact form to share your team, club, or program needs and we will follow up with pack and bulk options.

05

Can programs reorder during the season?

Yes. Stinger is designed for simple repeat ordering so teams can stay stocked through practices, tournaments, and camps.
